Who We Are

A Movement of Purpose,
Excellence & Transformation

ARETE Women's Generation is a faith-rooted, purpose-driven initiative dedicated to empowering women and girls to discover their uniqueness, unlock their purpose, cultivate exceptional character, and pursue intentional excellence.

Through community-based programs, digital platforms, and transformative experiences, ARETE equips women to thrive personally, spiritually, and socially — ultimately strengthening families, communities, and future generations.

Our Vision

To see women empowered to discover their uniqueness and unlock their purpose, cultivate exceptional character, and pursue lives of intentional excellence.

Our Mission

Rooted in faith and strengthened by community, we equip women with resources, guidance, and unwavering support to discover their unique purpose.

Meet the Founder

Faith Kumanyenu

Founder & Executive Director

Rooted in faith and driven by an unwavering passion for women's empowerment, Faith Kumanyenu founded ARETE Women's Generation with a bold vision: to see every woman discover her uniqueness, unlock her purpose, and pursue a life of intentional excellence.

What began as "The Lady's Hangout with Faith" — an intimate gathering of women sharing, healing, and growing together — has blossomed into a movement touching lives through six transformative programs spanning education, health, mentorship, and community impact.

“ Every woman carries a seed of greatness. Our mission is to nurture that seed until it blooms into something extraordinary. ”
What We Do

Our Programs

Six strategic programs addressing education, health, economic opportunity, and personal development.

Young Mothers

BLOOM

Supporting teenage pregnant girls and young mothers with vocational skills training, educational support, counseling, mentorship, and life-skills workshops.

Goal: Empower 500 young mothers annually
Health & Dignity

PadHer

Promoting menstrual health, dignity, and school attendance through sanitary pad distribution, hygiene education, and school outreach programs.

Goal: Reach 10,000 girls annually
YouTube Channel

Unfiltered

Sharing authentic stories of purpose, resilience, and transformation through weekly video episodes, interviews with impactful women, and faith-based teachings.

Goal: Grow to 50,000 subscribers
Social Media

Arete Inspirations

Daily encouragement and empowerment through inspirational posts, short teachings, testimonies, and community highlights across social platforms.

Goal: Build 20,000 engaged followers
Annual Event

Whole Again Conference

A brave space for healing, storytelling, and transformation through workshops on emotional healing, purpose, relationships, faith, and expert panel discussions.

Goal: Welcome 5,000 participants annually
Community Outreach

Love in Action

Serving vulnerable families and individuals with love and dignity through food distribution, clothing drives, and community service projects.

Goal: Support 2,000 families annually
